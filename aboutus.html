Description: This is a mobile app interface, thought to help the caregiver know about their patients condition/lociation. Added to this, both having access to the intarce, either one can send an sms to the 911 when an emergency ocurrs. This sms will contain extract of Patient Medical Record, information needed by the ER and paramedics, exact location of the request and brieve description of the emergency, saving time and marginal costs.

The purpose of Carelinked is to provide help for the ones who have someone under their supervision. Initially for the caregivers and the care provided to the elderly. Anyone with conditions such as Alzheimer's, children, people with history of heartattacks, strokes, recent major surjeries and anyone who needs an assistant eye on them.

The prototype Ideally the "patient/person in need" wears a device/wrist band which sends the heart rate, location and distres signal to the caregivers phone in case they for some reason separate for a moment. Still there are a few things to be studyed, measured and ponder.
