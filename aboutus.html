CARELINKED
Description: is an app thought to help caregivers and health care professionals by visualizing information such as: location, medical record and current health status of their patients. The paramedics will have this data when the 911 has been reached. Helping them make faster and even more cost effective decisions. A physical aid prototype is in the development process as well.

The purpose of Carelinked is to provide help for the ones who have someone under their supervision. Initially for the caregivers and the care providers. Patients with Alzheimer's, history of heartattacks, strokes, recent surjeries, etc.

The prototype The person under care has a portable device which sends the heart rate, location and distress signal to the caregiver's phone in case they separate far more than "X" in distance. Still, there are a few details that have to be measured and pondered.
